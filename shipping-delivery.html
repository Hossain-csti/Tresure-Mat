    • SHIPPING AND DELIVERY
    We want to give our customers flexibility when making any purchase from Aarong.com. Check out our payment options below for any order shipped within Bangladesh or outside of Bangladesh.

    If your delivery address is within Bangladesh, you may process your order using the full payment in advance or cash-on-delivery payment options.

    We will email you a system generated invoice/bill (based on your payment method) after your order has been confirmed. If you are a registered user, you can also find your invoice anytime from youruser account at Aarong.com.

    All payable amounts are in BDT and will consist of the following

    1. Subtotal of the product

    2. VAT (Finished textiles 7.5%, Non-textiles 5%, Jute products/books 0%)

    3. Shipping Cost

    Please note: If you are using an international credit card, you will be charged a converted amount in your local currency based on the conversion rate set by your financial institution. Please contact your respective financial institution to find out about current BDT conversion rates.

    If you are requesting delivery within Bangladesh, you will be able to make payments by using Visa, Mastercard, or AMEX debit/credit/pre-paid cards, mobile wallets (such as bKash, Rocket, SureCash, iPay, Upay, OK). Cash-on-Delivery option is available only for deliveries within Bangladesh.

    Please note Cash-on-Delivery option is not available for gold jewellery products above BDT 10000/-

    Our payment gateway partners are responsible for processing your payments through their secure payment gateways. Aarong.com will redirect you to their gateway when processing your payment during checkout.

    Currently, we use PortWallet as our main payment gateway for debit and credit card transactions. Also, BRAC Bank and Eastern Bank Limited payment gateways are available for use.

    If you are requesting delivery to a country other than Bangladesh, you will be able to make payments by using Visa or Mastercard debit/credit/pre-paid cards only.

    Our payment gateway partners are responsible for processing your payments through their secure payment gateways. Aarong.com will redirect you to their gateway when processing your payment during checkout.

    Credit card payments

    The total amount for your order will be charged to your credit card after your card has been approved at the payment gateway. Your order will be automatically cancelled if the amount charged exceeds the credit limit on your card or if the payment is not approved at the payment gateway for any other reason. If the payment is not accepted, please contact your concerned bank.

    Debit card payments

    The total billed amount for your order will be charged to your debit card after your card has been approved.

    Important Notice:

    Amount shown in each international webstore are estimated BDT converted prices based on the respective country's currency rates. The actual BDT amount will be shown at checkout which will be charged to your account.

    Please note, the total BDT amount will be converted by your local bank’s currency. Actual may vary based on your bank’s conversion rate.

    Promotional discount coupon is not available for any gold and gift card items. Any campaign discount is not applicable for gold jewellery, gift cards, Aarong Earth(if any).
